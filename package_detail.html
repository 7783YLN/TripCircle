About the trip

Embark on an unforgettable 6-day journey through two of Europe's most enchanting cities—Amsterdam and Paris. Begin your adventure in Amsterdam, where you'll explore the city's iconic canals on a scenic glass-roof cruise, wander through the historic Dam Square, and visit the vibrant Keukenhof Gardens (seasonal) or the charming miniature park of Madurodam. Experience Dutch culture with visits to a traditional wooden shoe factory and cheese farm.

Continue your journey to the romantic city of Paris, where you'll be captivated by world-famous landmarks including the Eiffel Tower (2nd level access), the majestic Arc de Triomphe, and the elegant Champs-Élysées. Enjoy a magical Seine River Cruise and spend a full day at the enchanting Disneyland Paris. En route, stop in Brussels to marvel at the Grand Place, the quirky Manneken Pis statue, and the futuristic Atomium.

This carefully curated tour blends culture, leisure, and romance, offering comfortable 4-star accommodations, delicious meals with Indian and Continental options, and the expertise of an English and Hindi-speaking tour manager. All driver and guide tips are included, ensuring a hassle-free and memorable European experience.

Day-wise Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Amsterdam

Arrive at Amsterdam Airport and transfer to your hotel. Evening at leisure to explore the city on your own. Overnight stay in Amsterdam.

Day 2: Amsterdam City Tour

After breakfast, embark on a guided city tour including a glass-roof canal cruise, visit to Dam Square, wooden shoe factory, and cheese farm. Visit Keukenhof Gardens (seasonal) or Madurodam. Dinner and overnight in Amsterdam.

Day 3: Amsterdam to Paris via Brussels

Check out and drive to Paris with a stop in Brussels. Visit the Grand Place, Manneken Pis statue, and photo stop at Atomium. Enjoy Indian lunch in Brussels. Continue to Paris for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 4: Paris City Tour

After breakfast, enjoy a guided city tour of Paris covering Champs-Élysées, Arc de Triomphe, Place de la Concorde, and more. Visit the Eiffel Tower (2nd level). Evening Seine River Cruise. Dinner and overnight in Paris.

Day 5: Disneyland Paris

Full day at Disneyland Paris. Explore the magical world of Disney with thrilling rides, shows, and attractions. Dinner and overnight in Paris.

Day 6: Departure from Paris

After breakfast, check out and transfer to Paris Airport for your return flight home with wonderful memories.
